Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation Time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt to taste
  • Pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: Cheese, diced vegetables, cooked meat, herbs

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs: In a bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until the mix is well blended.
  2. Heat the pan: Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.
  3. Cook the omelette: Once the butter is melted and bubbling, pour in the eggs. Tilt the pan to allow the eggs to coat the surface.
  4. Add optional fillings: While the eggs begin to set, add your choice of fillings to one side of the omelette.
  5. Fold and serve: Gently fold the omelette in half and cook for another minute. Transfer to a plate and serve hot.
